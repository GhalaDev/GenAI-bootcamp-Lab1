What I learned about Hugging Face: It serves as a collaborative hub for hosting, sharing, and discovering open-source AI models and datasets.

One AI project idea I want to build during the bootcamp: AI-Powered Custom Board & Card Game Creator

The Concept: A web platform that allows families, friends, or educators to design their own custom card or board game within minutes based on any theme they choose (e.g., Space Exploration, Mystery/Detective, Fantasy).

AI's Role:

Text Generation (LLM): Generates the game rules, lore, and character dialogue/challenges for the cards.

Image Generation: Automatically designs distinct visual artwork for each card and the main game board based on the selected theme.

Why it stands out: It moves away from standard chatbots, focusing on a highly creative, end-to-end tangible product that users can view digitally or export.
